BYU's President Announces Lowering of ROC Pass Price for Students

By Lehi Jenkins

President Reese discussing the ROC pass with Lehi Jenkins.

President C. Shane Reese has announced in a recent interview after the recent commitments of athletes to BYU the lowering of the ROC Pass price. The news, breaking just days after Egor Demin declared for the NBA Draft, has sparked a wave of excitement and relief among BYU students.
